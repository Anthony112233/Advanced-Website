My Father

My Father works as a gastroenterology located in Glendora California. He was born in Syria in 1964 and immigrated to America to live out his dream. He attended the University of Allepo Faculty of Medicine in Haleb Syria. After immigrating to America, he passed an exam and did his fellowship at Wayne State University. He later moved to Oakland and then to Southern California Los Angeles Area to begin his practice.

My Mother

My Mom is a homemaker. She had the hardest job in the world raising three kids. She was born in Syria as well and immigrated to the united states as a young girl. She then married my father and settled down in California. My mother never had a chance to finish college and now attends Citrus college where she works towards an associate degree.


My Brothers

He attended Foothill Country Day School than the Webb School and then Loyola Marymount University, majoring in economics and political Science. Andrew was born in Oakdale in 1999 where he also attended the Webb Schools, and now begins as a freshman at USC majoring in Human Biology.
